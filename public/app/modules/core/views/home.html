What is Fusion Fizz?

Fusion Fizz is a collection of trainings, resources and techniques for developing robust, scalable solutions using Node.js and AngularJS.

Fusion Fizz's goal help educate people, companies and other interested parties in technology that is quickly overtaking the software development landscape.
